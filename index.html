The Garden Chapel

We're getting married in the beautiful Willow Creek Garden, where our ceremony will take place beneath an ancient oak tree. The venue features manicured lawns, a stone fountain, and a rustic chapel made of reclaimed wood.

Complimentary shuttle service will be available from the nearby parking lot. The venue is fully accessible with ramps and designated parking spaces for guests with mobility needs.

Our Story

When we're not exploring new restaurants or hiking local trails, you'll find us curled up with a good book or planning our next adventure. Our pets, a mischievous golden retriever named Max, will be joining us on the big day!
